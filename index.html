Biography

Zhen Liu received his B.S. and M.S. degrees from the School of Computer Science at Sichuan University in 2018 and 2021, supervised by Prof. Jiancheng Lv. Now he is a full time researcher in MEGVII Research, supervised by Prof. Shuaicheng Liu. His research interests include Computer Vision and Computer Graphics.
